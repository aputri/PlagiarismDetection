In modern times, it can be argued that computer science is one of the most
valuable studies to learn. Currently, nearly every major industry involves
computers in some form, thus providing a massive variety of job opportunities.
Likewise, because technology is continuously progressing, the future of
computer science will undoubtedly lead to many new and exciting opportunities.
Furthermore, programming and developing can serve as a creative outlet, while
still being based around logic and algorithms. Computer science is both an
interesting and rewarding field to study.
